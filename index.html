About

With over a decade of experience in eLearning development, graphic design, and creative problem-solving, Jaden specializes in transforming complex information into immersive, learner-centered experiences. From interactive government training to gamified healthcare simulations, Jaden brings a learner-first mindset to every project. Passionate about accessibility, innovation, and purposeful design, they thrive in collaborative spaces where curiosity meets strategy — and learning becomes unforgettable.

  • Tools: Articulate Storyline/Rise, Adobe CC, Figma, Captivate, Canva, Google Suite, Microsoft 365 Suite, ChatGPT, CoPilot
  • Strengths: eLearning development, Graphic design, Video editing, Agile methodology, Asset creation, Communication, Problem solving, Process workflows
  • Industries: Education systems, Entertainment, Government, Healthcare, HR/Payroll, SaaS

What I’m looking for
